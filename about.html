My Story

My name is Gabriel and I’m a junior UX Designer, originally from Venezuela, living and creating in Madrid, Spain.

With +7 years of experience in the public sector as project manager and communication, working and designing public services, I faced so many times how these essential services where not user-centered and where so difficult to use. That’s why I decided to study UX/UI and learn how to improve the procces of elaborating public services. The path took me to take the decision to work not only for the public sector but also for the private sector. Im focus on creating products and services with the end user in mind.

I’m determinated in creating and improving processes, simplifying the complex and solving problems. I’m a “doer” and a dreamer. Do you want to take your product or service to the next level? Let’s talk!
